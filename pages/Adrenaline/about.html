Background

Adrenaline is a game made by me (MagpieTaxi) as a passion project, and is inspired by many other parkor games such as Mirror's Edge, Huzell's Parkour (Legacy and Reborn), District Cascade, and the real-world sport of parkour. Adrenaline was initially started around mid 2023 as a quick project because of my dissatisfaction with other popular parkour games. Over time, it grew into something much more than i initially intended it to be. As of 5/14/24, it is currently worked on by me and my friend Galaxial, and is under heavy development.
